PERSONAL EXPENSE TRACKER

1. PROJECT TITLE

Personal Expense Tracker

---

2. PROBLEM STATEMENT

Managing personal finances can be challenging, especially for students and individuals who handle multiple daily expenses. Many people fail to keep track of their spending, leading to poor budgeting, unnecessary expenses, and difficulty in achieving financial goals.

Traditional methods such as manual record-keeping are time-consuming and prone to errors. There is a need for a simple and user-friendly system that allows users to record, monitor, and analyze their expenses efficiently.

The Personal Expense Tracker addresses this problem by providing a digital platform to manage income and expenses, helping users maintain better financial control and make informed spending decisions.

---

3. PROJECT OBJECTIVE

The main objective of the Personal Expense Tracker is to help users effectively manage their personal finances by recording and monitoring their daily expenses.

Specific Objectives:

• To provide an easy way to add and manage expenses.

• To categorize expenses for better financial analysis.

• To maintain a record of spending activities.

• To calculate total expenses automatically.

• To help users understand their spending habits.

• To promote better budgeting and financial planning.

---

4. MODULES LIST

Module 1: User Interface

- Provides a simple and interactive interface for users.
- Allows users to enter expense details easily.

Module 2: Expense Management

- Add new expenses.
- Edit existing expenses.
- Delete expense records.

Module 3: Category Management

- Organize expenses into categories such as Food, Transport, Shopping, Education, and Others.
- Helps users analyze spending patterns.

Module 4: Expense Tracking

- Maintains a complete record of all expenses.
- Displays expense history.

Module 5: Budget Analysis

- Calculates total expenses.
- Provides spending summaries.
- Helps users compare expenses across categories.

Module 6: Report Generation

- Generates expense reports.
- Displays financial information in an organized format.

The Personal Expense Tracker will provide a simple and effective solution for managing personal finances. Users will be able to track their expenses, analyze spending patterns, and make better financial decisions through an easy-to-use web application.
