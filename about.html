About Our Website

Personal Expense Tracker is a simple web application designed to help users monitor their daily expenses and manage their finances efficiently.


Users can add expenses, view expense history, monitor spending patterns, and manage their monthly budgets.


Features

  • Track daily expenses
  • Monitor monthly spending
  • Manage personal budget
  • View expense history
  • Simple and responsive design
